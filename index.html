STUDIO 129
FITNESS BY APPOINTMENT

IT STARTED WITH AN IDEA...

When we conceived Studio 129, we had one thing in mind: an escape from the overcrowded, noisy, and intimidating environment of your average gym experience. We wanted something much more personal and welcoming; an environment to spark passion within our clientele to bring them to new heights. Studio 129 is first and foremost a space where you can go to experience freedom and further your fitness goals without judgement or anxiety. We strive to bring out the very best in our customers: We work our hardest so you can achieve the results you desire. From weight loss to athletic training, our staff has countless years of experience and the right mindset to help you start your personal fitness journey.

This is YOUR experience

YOU ARE IN GOOD HANDS

Our trainers are some of the most trusted within the fitness community today and to help you achieve all of your goals. With over 10 years of experience each, our trainers will provide the motivation and inspiration needed to go above and beyond!

MICHAEL PERRUCCI

Michael Perrucci is a man who needs little introduction. From his tenure spearheading "The Gym" of Locust Valley (later called Elite Fitness) in 2003, he has become a staple of the North Shore fitness community. Perrucci has built lasting relationships between clients of all ages for over 15 years and has done so with his can do attitude and welcoming personality. Michael is Certified by the American Aerobic Association International & International Sports Medicine Association. DEBBIE CARMICHAEL

Having been Certified by The American Council on Exercise, Debbie's training experience spans nearly 20 years. Focusing on body toning and sculpting, Debbie combines weight and strength, functional, and sports training into her programs. Post injury and senior members also rely heavily on Debbie's knowledge to design a program specifically for them and is conscious of their physical restrictions. DEEDEE HOUCK

The first thing anyone will mention about DeeDee Houck when asked about her would be her upbeat attitude. Not a day goes by where she is nothing but a ray of in the gym. DeeDee has lived the fitness industry for 30 years as a certified personal trainer and fitness instructor. She specializes in core strength and posture corrective training to achieve the ultimate fitness program. DeeDee works with all ages as well as those recovering due to injury or disabilities. DeeDee believes in creating possibilities; strong core, strong life!
