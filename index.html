STUDIO 129
FITNESS BY APPOINTMENT

IT STARTED WITH AN IDEA...

When we conceived Studio 129, we had one thing in mind: an escape from the overcrowded, noisy, and intimidating environment of your average gym experience. We wanted something much more personal and welcoming; an environment to spark passion within our clientele to bring them to new heights. Studio 129 is first and foremost a space where you can go to experience freedom and further your fitness goals without judgement or anxiety. We strive to bring out the very best in our customers: We work our hardest so you can achieve the results you desire. From weight loss to athletic training, or staff has countless years of experience and the right mindset to help you start your personal fitness journey.
