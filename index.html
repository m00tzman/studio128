HOME SWEET HOME

Located in the heart of Locust Valley, New York, Studio 129 is a unique fitness studio equipped with everything you need for a private personal training experience. From strength training to weight loss, general fitness to rehabilitation; Studio 129 has all of the amenities to serve your needs.

YOU ARE IN GOOD HANDS

Our team cosnsists of some of the most restpected members within the fitness community and cover a variety of expertises and practices. With years of experience, our trainers can provide the insight and inspiration to help go above and beyond your fitness goals!

MICHAEL PERRUCCI

Michael Perrucci is a man who needs little introduction. From his tenure spearheading "The Gym" of Locust Valley (later called Elite Fitness) in 2003, he has become a staple of the North Shore fitness community. For over fifteen years, Perrucci has built lasting relationships between clients of all ages for over and has done so with his can-do attitude and motivating personality. His training philosophy encompasses not only exercise but encourages overall changes to promote personal well being and confidence. Michael provides the push that is needed to go above and beyond your goals and expectations! Perrucci is Certified by the American Aerobic Association International & International Sports Medicine Association.

DEBBIE CARMICHAEL

Having been Certified by The American Council on Exercise, Debbie's training experience spans nearly 20 years. Focusing on body toning and sculpting, Debbie combines weight and strength, functional, and sports training into her programs. Post injury and senior members also rely heavily on Debbie's knowledge to design a program specifically for them and is conscious of their physical restrictions. Debbie takes consideration with her clients and makes sure to help accomodate to their needs.

DEEDEE HOUCK

The first thing anyone will mention about DeeDee Houck when asked about her would be her upbeat attitude. Not a day goes by where she is nothing but a ray sunshine of in the gym. DeeDee has lived the fitness industry for 30 years as a certified personal trainer and fitness instructor. She specializes in core strength and posture corrective training to achieve the ultimate fitness program. DeeDee works with all ages as well as those recovering due to injury or disabilities. DeeDee believes in creating possibilities; strong core, strong life!

IT STARTED WITH AN IDEA...

When we conceived Studio 129, we had one thing in mind: an escape from the overcrowded, noisy, and intimidating environment of your average gym experience. We wanted something much more personal and welcoming; an environment to spark passion within our clientele to bring them to new heights. Studio 129 is first and foremost a space where you can go to experience freedom and further your fitness goals without judgement or anxiety. We strive to bring out the very best in our customers: We work our hardest so you can achieve the results you desire. From weight loss to athletic training, our staff has countless years of experience and the right mindset to help you start your personal fitness journey.
