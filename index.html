YOU ARE IN GOOD HANDS

Our team consists of some of the most respected members within the fitness community and cover a variety of methodologies and practices. With years of experience, our trainers can provide the insight and inspiration to help go above and beyond your fitness goals!

  • MICHAEL PERRUCCI

    Michael Perrucci is a man who needs little introduction. From his tenure spearheading "The Gym" of Locust Valley (later called Elite Fitness) in 2003, he has become a staple of the North Shore fitness community. For over fifteen years, Perrucci has built lasting relationships between clients of all ages and has done so with his can-do attitude and motivating personality. His training philosophy encompasses not only exercise but encourages overall changes to promote personal well being and confidence. Michael provides the push that is needed to go above and beyond your goals and expectations! Perrucci is Certified by the American Aerobic Association International & International Sports Medicine Association.

  • DEBBIE CARMICHAEL

    Having been Certified by The American Council on Exercise, Debbie's training experience spans nearly 20 years. Focusing on body toning and sculpting, Debbie combines weight and strength, functional, and sports training into her programs. Post injury and senior members also rely heavily on Debbie's knowledge to design a program specifically for them and is conscious of their physical restrictions. Debbie takes consideration with her clients and makes sure to help accommodate to their needs.

  • DEEDEE HOUCK

    DeeDee Houck has been in the fitness industry for 30 years as a certified personal trainer specializing in posture alignment with core and balance training for those looking for optimal fitness as well as building core strength for any situation life may bring. Her upbeat attitude motivates all who train with her, helping to create whatever goal they might have and turning it into a way of life. DeeDee's training has a flair of core fusion and bar technique as it is a part of her mantra: Strong Core, Strong Life!

IT STARTED WITH AN IDEA...

When we conceived Studio 129, we had one thing in mind: an escape from the overcrowded, noisy, and intimidating environment of your average gym experience. We wanted something much more personal and welcoming; an environment to spark passion within our clientele to bring them to new heights. Studio 129 is first and foremost a space where you can go to experience freedom and further your fitness goals without judgement or anxiety. We strive to bring out the very best in our customers: We work our hardest so you can achieve the results you desire. From weight loss to athletic training, our staff has countless years of experience and the right mindset to help you start your personal fitness journey.
